About Mentor Me

Real world experience from real people.

Mentor Me lets you lend your business skills to an entrepreneurs across the globe. New entrepreneurs often have a lot of questions, and don’t always have a mentor to ask. As an entrepreneur, whenever you need business advice, our volunteers are happy to help. By posting your questions, you can communicate directly and solve a problem. As an experience business owner you can help pay it forward just by installing the Mentor Me app.

  • Quick answers.
  • Real industry leaders.
  • Free support, for your life long journey.
